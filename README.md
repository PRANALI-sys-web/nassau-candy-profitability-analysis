# 🍫 Nassau Candy Product Line Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

An end-to-end **Data Analytics and Business Intelligence project** focused on analyzing product line profitability and sales performance for **Nassau Candy Distributor**.

The project transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights by analyzing key financial metrics such as **Sales, Cost, Gross Profit, and Margin Percentage**. An interactive Streamlit dashboard was developed to help identify profitable products, understand margin trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Businesses need visibility into product performance to optimize pricing strategies, reduce cost inefficiencies, and improve profitability.

This project answers key business questions:

* Which products generate the highest profit?
* Which product lines have low profitability?
* How does sales volume impact profit?
* Which categories contribute most to overall revenue?
* Where can margin improvement opportunities be found?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Identify top-performing and low-performing products
* Analyze profitability across product divisions
* Study the relationship between units sold and profit
* Perform Pareto analysis (80/20 rule) to identify major profit contributors
* Build an interactive dashboard for business insights
